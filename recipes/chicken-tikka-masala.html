Chicken Tikka Masala

Description

Chicken Tikka Masala is a beloved dish featuring tender chunks of roasted marinated chicken in a spiced curry sauce. This British-Indian creation combines the smoky flavors of tandoori chicken with a rich, creamy tomato-based sauce that's perfectly balanced with aromatic spices.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs boneless chicken thighs, cut into chunks
  • 1 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken chunks in yogurt, lemon juice, half the garam masala, cumin, cayenne, minced garlic, and ginger for at least 1 hour.
  2. Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C). Thread chicken onto skewers and bake for 15-20 minutes until cooked through.
  3. In a large skillet, melt butter and sauté diced onion until golden, about 8 minutes.
  4. Add remaining garlic, ginger, and spices. Cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  5. Add crushed tomatoes and simmer for 10-15 minutes until thickened.
  6. Stir in heavy cream and remaining garam masala. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Add the cooked chicken to the sauce and simmer for 5 minutes.
  8. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with basmati rice and naan bread.
